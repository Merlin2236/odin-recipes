Island Water

Description

This classic island drink is kissed from hawaiin water falls. With a splash of rum and fresh mango juice, it is likely to tickle your fancy.

Ingredients

  • Hawaiin Rum
  • Fresh Mango Juice
  • Hawaiin Water
  • Malibu Rum
  • 1 Lime Slice
  • 1 sphere ice cube

Steps

  1. Add the 2 Rums
  2. Add the mango juice and water
  3. Pore over the ice cube and add the lime
  4. Get ready to have a good time!!!
