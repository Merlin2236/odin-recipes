Hawaiin Poke Bowl

Description

This hawaiin poke bowl is a delicious meal to enjoy on the beach. The fresh caught ahi tuna is served on a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna
  • Basmati Rice
  • Avacado
  • Pickled Mango
  • Special Sauce

Steps

  1. Place the tune on a bed of rice
  2. Slice an avacado and layer it over the top
  3. Neatly slice the pickled mango over the top as well
  4. Drizzle the sauce over everything and Enjoy!!!
