Sardines and Crackers

Description

Enjoy fresh caught sardines on a salty cracker. This appatizer will tickle your fancy and is a great snack to enjoy at D'lish at the Beach

Ingredients

  • Fesh Sardines
  • Salty Cracker
  • Pepper
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Open the sardines and place them on a cracker
  2. Sprinkle pepper over the top
  3. Squeeze the lemon over the sardine
  4. Enjoy!!!
